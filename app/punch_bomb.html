hot potato — how to play

  • soup is the potato. punch him and he flies to the next player
  • when he lands on you, you have ~2 seconds to punch him back — too slow and he blows up on you
  • every punch adds points to your stash. harder punches score more, so wind up if you dare
  • the more he's been punched this round, the closer he is to going off. watch him flicker between colors — cream, orange, red — but the actual boom point is random
  • if he explodes on you, your stash is gone — everyone else banks theirs
  • too scared? hold still (or hit eat it) to eat the punch: no boom, but you forfeit your whole stash
  • first to 100 banked points wins, or the biggest bank after 5 rounds
